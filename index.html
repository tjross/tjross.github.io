How It Works

1. Submit Your Idea

2. Meet Your Designer

3. View Digital Preview

4. Hold In Your Hands

1

Submit Your Design Idea

Get Inspired and Tell Us What You Want to Make

Fill out a design brief and add pictures and/or sketches to an Idea Board to communicate the vision for your product. If you need some inspiration, let us know, we can help!

Start Collaborating

Meet Your Designer and Start Your Project

We will assign you a designer from our network of world-class product designers. Once you start your project, you will be directed to our design center where you'll communicate directly with your designer.

3

View Digital Preview

Bring Your Idea to Life on Screen Before Making Your Order

As soon as you and your designer agree on a design, your designer will send across a life-like digital preview of your product. Iterate on your product design until you are in love.

Hold It In Your Hands

Choose Material and Ship the Design to Your Door

Once you agree on a final design, just choose the material and finish you want your product in and get it delivered to your door. You can literally hold your idea in your hands.
